## 2. Scope and Carve-Outs

### In Scope
- ALdeci platform software stack (all `suite-*` packages)
- Authentication, authorization, session management, RBAC
- Cryptographic key management and FIPS 140-2 encryption subsystems
- Audit logging, evidence chain, and compliance evidence generation
- Vulnerability scanning, CTEM pipeline, and AutoFix engine
- Incident response and alert management
- Network access control configurations (Docker/Kubernetes runtime)

### Out of Scope — INHERITED (Customer Responsibility)
- Physical facility security (A.7.1–A.7.14 physical controls at customer data center)
- Corporate HR/personnel policies (A.6.1–A.6.6 people controls)
- Underlying IaaS infrastructure when cloud-hosted
- Supplier contracts and third-party due diligence at organizational level
