## Three Proof Bullets

- **GDPR and HIPAA Privacy Rule mapping with automated evidence generation.** privacy_gdpr_engine.py maps detected PII findings to GDPR Articles (5, 6, 13, 17, 25, 30, 32, 35) and privacy_impact_assessment_engine.py runs automated DPIA workflows for new processing activities. data_privacy_engine.py provides the PII inventory layer — scanning codebases, APIs, and data stores for personal data exposure. Compliance evidence is generated automatically: cryptographically signed bundles with WORM retention. (Source: suite-core/core/privacy_gdpr_engine.py, data_privacy_engine.py, privacy_impact_assessment_engine.py)
- **PII detection integrated with the full Brain Pipeline — not a standalone scanner.** When ALdeci's SAST, DAST, Secrets, or LLM Monitor engines detect personal data exposure, findings flow through all 12 Brain Pipeline steps — including AI consensus triage, MPTE exploit verification, and AutoFix remediation. Privacy violations are not just flagged; they are prioritized, verified, and fixed with the same rigor as security vulnerabilities. (Source: docs/CTEM_PLUS_IDENTITY.md §12-Step Brain Pipeline, §Native Security Engines)
- **Quantum-secure evidence satisfies multi-jurisdiction regulatory scrutiny.** Every privacy compliance action — PII finding, DPIA completion, DSAR fulfillment, consent record — is captured in an evidence bundle signed with FIPS 204 ML-DSA (post-quantum) + RSA hybrid. 7-year WORM retention covers GDPR Article 30 record-keeping requirements. Evidence bundles are self-contained: regulators receive a verifiable artifact, not a manual export. (Source: docs/CTEM_PLUS_IDENTITY.md §Quantum-Secure Evidence, §Compliance Framework Coverage)
